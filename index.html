Tulkarm

A Palestinian agricultural city located in the northwest of the West Bank. Its average height above sea level is about 100 meters, and it is about 15 kilometers from the shore of the Mediterranean Sea. Its original name is "Turkarm" and it was called that because of its rich, fertile land until the 18th century AD, when it was changed to "Tulkarm".
